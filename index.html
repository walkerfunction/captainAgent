SRH vs LSG

10th Match, IPL 2026 · Rajiv Gandhi Intl. Stadium, Hyderabad · Apr 5

Sunrisers Hyderabad welcome Lucknow Super Giants to the Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium for what promises to be a lopsided affair on paper, though cricket rarely follows the script. SRH arrive riding high after a commanding 65-run demolition in their last outing, their batting lineup clicking into the kind of ruthless gear that defined their devastating 2025 campaign. Lucknow, meanwhile, stumble into Hyderabad having lost to Delhi Capitals and carrying the weight of a dismal run stretching back to last season, where four defeats in their final five matches exposed a brittle middle order.

The head-to-head tilts firmly toward Hyderabad, who chased down LSG's total with six wickets to spare when these sides last met. Pat Cummins continues to be the tournament's most reliable wicket-taker with 77 scalps since 2020, and Marco Jansen's 74 give SRH a pace attack that thrives under lights on this surface.

Rishabh Pant remains Lucknow's great hope, but Cummins has already claimed his wicket this cycle, and that early psychological edge matters in a contest of fine margins.

Key factor to watch: Adam Zampa operating in the middle overs against Lucknow's aggressive but inconsistent middle order. If Zampa can strangle the run rate between overs 10 and 15, SRH's seamers will finish the job. Hyderabad start as overwhelming favourites, and it will take something special from Pant to change that narrative.
